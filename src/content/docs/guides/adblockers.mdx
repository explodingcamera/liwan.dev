---
title: Ad Blockers
---

Some visitors use content blockers that block analytics scripts by pattern, domain name, or request path. This can make analytics undercount traffic.

If you want to reduce accidental blocking:

- **Use a neutral subdomain**: Names like `analytics.example.com` are more likely to match blocklists. A neutral name such as `a.example.com` or `liwan.example.com` may be less likely to be blocked.
- **Serve the script from your own domain**: Liwan is self-hosted, so the tracking script is already served from infrastructure you control.
